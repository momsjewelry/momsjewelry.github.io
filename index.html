the artist

meet my mom:
Vika

My mom is the best. She has five daughters who fill her life with joy and adventures. She enjoys making clay earrings and trinkets in her free time, though she rarely has the chance to do so.

In the spring of 2022, she and her family had to flee Ukraine. They settled in Belgium, but their lives are still full of uncertainty and worry.

I made this website to help my mom move forward with her favorite hobby and let other people see her creations and support her.

  • every piece is original and filled with my mom's love
  • she makes earrings, brooches, cup decorations, and many more
  • she will be happy to make something special for you!
